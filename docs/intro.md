# Introduction to Physical AI & Humanoid Robotics

Welcome to the comprehensive guide on **Physical AI & Humanoid Robotics**. This interactive book explores the fascinating world of embodied artificial intelligence and how it enables humanoid robots to perceive, understand, and interact with the physical world.

## What You'll Learn

This book covers the essential components required to understand and develop humanoid robots:

- **The Robotic Nervous System**: How ROS 2 provides the communication backbone for robot systems
- **The Digital Twin**: Using Gazebo and Unity for robot simulation and testing
- **The AI-Robot Brain**: NVIDIA Isaac™ platform for AI-powered robot control
- **Vision-Language-Action (VLA)**: How robots integrate perception, understanding, and action

### Target Audience

This book is designed for:
- Computer science and engineering students
- Developers working in robotics and AI
- Researchers interested in embodied AI
- Anyone curious about humanoid robot control systems

## How to Use This Book
